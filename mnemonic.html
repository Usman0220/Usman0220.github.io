Medical Mnemonics

A collection of mnemonics to help remember important medical information.

Anatomy

Cranial Nerves

"On Occasion, Our Trusty Truck Acts Funny, Very Good Vehicle AnyHow."

Olfactory, Optic, Oculomotor, Trochlear, Trigeminal, Abducens, Facial, Vestibulocochlear, Glossopharyngeal, Vagus, Accessory, Hypoglossal

Bones in the Orbit

"My Little Eye Sits in the orbit"

Maxilla, Lacrimal, Ethmoid, Sphenoid

Clinical Assessment

Stroke Symptoms (BE-FAST)

"BE-FAST"

Balance off, Eyes (blurred or loss of vision), Face drooping, Arm weakness, Speech difficulty, Time to call 911

Newborn Assessment (APGAR)

"APGAR"

Appearance, Pulse, Grimace, Activity, and Respiration

Diseases and Conditions

Causes of Pancreatitis

"I GET SMASHED"

Idiopathic, Gallstones, Ethanol, Trauma, Steroids, Mumps, Autoimmune, Scorpion sting, Hypercalcemia/hypertriglyceridemia, ERCP, and Drugs

Symptoms of Depression

"SIG E CAPS"

Sleep, Interest, Guilt, Energy, Concentration, Appetite, Psychomotor agitation or retardation, and Suicidal ideation
