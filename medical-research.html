Research & Evidence Synthesis

Medical Research & Meta-Analysis

Rigorous systematic review and meta-analysis form the backbone of evidence-based medicine. My research focuses on synthesizing clinical data to guide treatment decisions and identify knowledge gaps.

Research Interests

  • Systematic Reviews — Comprehensive literature synthesis following PRISMA guidelines.
  • Meta-Analysis — Quantitative pooling of clinical trial data to estimate treatment effects.
  • Clinical Epidemiology — Study design, bias assessment, and methodological rigor.

“Evidence-based medicine is the conscientious use of current best evidence in making decisions about patient care.”

Current Projects

Conducting meta-analytic synthesis of clinical outcomes across multi-center trials, with emphasis on heterogeneity analysis and publication bias detection.
