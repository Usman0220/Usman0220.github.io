Artificial Intelligence in Clinical Medicine

The integration of artificial intelligence into medical practice is transforming how we diagnose, treat, and manage disease. My work focuses on leveraging computer vision and machine learning to augment clinical decision-making.

Key Areas

  • Medical Imaging — Applying deep learning to radiology and pathology images for pattern recognition.
  • Predictive Analytics — Developing risk stratification models from electronic health records.
  • Clinical Decision Support — Building tools that integrate AI recommendations into physician workflows.

“Bridging clinical intuition with computational precision to improve patient outcomes.”

Current Work

Exploring Python-based computer vision pipelines for medical image analysis, with a focus on interpretability and clinical validation. Collaborating with clinicians to ensure AI tools meet real-world bedside needs.
