Digital Health

Health-Tech Innovation

Technology has the power to transform healthcare delivery. My work in health-tech focuses on prototyping digital solutions that integrate seamlessly into clinical environments.

Areas of Focus

  • Clinical Workflow Automation — Streamlining documentation and data collection for clinicians.
  • Digital Health Tools — Building web-based applications for medical history taking and assessment.
  • AI Integration — Prototyping machine learning workflows for point-of-care decision support.

“Technology should serve the clinician, not replace them. The goal is better patient outcomes through smarter tools.”

Projects

Developed MedHistory, a clinical history-taking assistant that generates structured documentation for Surgery, Medicine, Paediatrics, and Gynaecology & Obstetrics specialties. Designed for offline use with print/PDF export and JSON data export.
